About HOREMOW

Holiness Revival Movement Worldwide - Bringing Christ's Sheep Back to True Holiness

"Follow peace with all men, and holiness, without which no man shall see the Lord"

Hebrews 12:14

HOLINESS REVIVAL MINISTRY WORLDWIDE (also known as Holiness Revival Movement Worldwide - HOREMOW) is a non-denominational Ministry given to the promotion of biblical Righteousness and Holiness in Churches and Nations of the world.

Our Foundation

The ministry started with 22 people in 2009 and was officially inaugurated in 2010 under the divine guidance of our Lord, Savior, and King Jesus Christ. Founded by Pastor Paul Rika, HOREMOW is ordained in these last days to bring His sheep back to true holiness and righteousness.

Our Ultimate Goal

To evangelize the whole world in preparation for the rapture through the message of biblical holiness and righteousness.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the infallible authority of the Holy Bible as the final guide for Christian life and doctrine.

Our core belief is in Holiness without which no man shall see the Lord (Hebrews 12:14).

The Holy Trinity

+

We believe that the Holy Trinity consists of GOD Almighty, The Father, JESUS CHRIST the son of GOD, and Holy Spirit; all the three are one indivisible in Godhead and yet distinct in names.

Virgin Birth, Sinless Life & Resurrection

+

We believe that JESUS CHRIST was born by a virgin, lived a sinless life, was crucified, died on the cross, buried, and rose triumphantly from the grave on the third day. He ascended into heaven and is coming back to take believers home.

Repentance & Restitution

+

We believe in the utter depravity of human nature and the necessity for repentance. Restitution is the act of restoring to rightful owners what has been stolen or defrauded, making amends for wrongs perpetrated.

Justification & Sanctification

+

We believe in justification by faith in Jesus Christ and sanctification as being set apart as sacred and holy through God's grace, achieved after being born again.

Water Baptism & Lord's Supper

+

We believe in water baptism by immersion and the regular observance of the Lord's Supper in remembrance of Christ's atoning death and resurrection.
